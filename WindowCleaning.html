How I Work

I work from ground level using a carbon fibre telescopic pole, this means it is both safe for me, and your property allowing cleaning up to 3 stories, including most velux windows.

I use pure deionised water which I filter at my home and transfer to a tank in my van.
As all impurities have been removed, your windows will be left spotless and crystal clear without the need for any chemicals.
